About

Building things that matter, with people who care.

I'm Richard Wells — a builder, thinker, and maker operating at the intersection of technology, design, and human possibility. With a background that spans product development, creative direction, and strategic problem-solving, I bring ideas from concept to reality with clarity and craft.

My work is driven by a simple belief: the best tools amplify people, they don't replace them. Whether I'm developing a new product, forging a partnership, or exploring an untested idea, I bring the same rigour and curiosity to every project.

Based in Wales, working globally. Always open to ideas worth building.
